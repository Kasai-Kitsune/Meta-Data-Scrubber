What is not supported?

DNG and other RAW camera files are not processed. GIF, animated WebP, documents, archives, and uncommon media containers are also excluded in this version. Unsupported files are rejected rather than falsely reported as clean.

2. Cleaning mode

Roughly, how does cleaning work?

Images: the browser decodes the visible pixels, draws them to a fresh canvas, and exports a new image of the same type. Metadata such as EXIF is not intentionally copied. JPEG and WebP output is encoded at high quality.

Video/audio, Standard: FFmpeg rewrites the media container, copies normal media streams, and does not map global metadata or chapters. Cover art, attachments, data streams, and subtitle streams are not copied.

Video/audio, Maximum: FFmpeg decodes and re-encodes normal audio/video streams in the same general file type, while omitting metadata, chapters, subtitles, attachments, and data streams.

No general-purpose cleaner can honestly guarantee removal of every unknown or proprietary structure. The result panel reports the operation performed and any warnings.
